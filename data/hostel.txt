Hostel Life at Bennett University Bennett University offers thoughtfully designed on-campus hostels for boys and girls, located within the campus , to ensure a safe, comfortable, and modern living experience for students. Caring wardens and vigilant security personnel create a supportive environment, allowing students to focus on their academics. >The hostels can accommodate up to 10,000 students and feature well-furnished triple-sharing rooms equipped with air conditioning, comfortable beds, and ample storage. Hygiene is a key priority, with regularly cleaned common washrooms and well-maintained facilities. Recreational common rooms with indoor games and entertainment options encourage relaxation and community bonding. With round-the-clock operations, power backup through DG sets, centralised temperature control, routine maintenance, and a dedicated safety team conducting daily checks, the campus supports seamless living. Students also have access to sports, wellness maintained by MAX Healthcare , and fitness facilities, promoting a holistic and enriching campus life. Hostel accommodation is available on a first-come-first-served basis. Whether a student resides on campus or is a day scholar, they are immersed in a dynamic environment rich with cultural, club-based, and community activities. The university is committed to the holistic well-being and development of every student—both inside and outside the classroom. The campus culture strikes a thoughtful balance between academics and recreation, offering a space where education and entertainment thrive side by side. Students enjoy access to world-class amenities that support a wide range of extracurricular interests, particularly in sports and recreation. The campus houses a well-equipped sports store stocked with high-quality gear for football, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, pool, chess, carrom, badminton, cricket, and athletics. From the energy of cultural festivals to the excitement of college fests, the spirit of sporting competitions and 40+ student-led academic, cultural, technical & recreational clubs offer students a stage to demonstrate their talents, leadership, and teamwork.
